Miranda's Top Three Favourite Movies

The movies I just can't stop watching

Moulin Rouge

Freedom, beauty, truth and love! What more could anyone ask for but a beautiful romance between a penniless writer and a courtesan. Plus, the eye-catching direction of Baz Lurman and some incredible musical hits.

John Wick

Spoiler Alert!!...His dog is murdered and he goes on an epic mission to get revenge. I can never get enough of this unique story and the action it contains. The best action movie of all time!

Shrek

This is the perfect movie to watch when you are sick, depressed, or needing a tight warm hug. Great sounds, amazing characters, brilliant humour and a storyline like no other... and yet, like many others. A classic!
